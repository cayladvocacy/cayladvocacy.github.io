About Us
Welcome to the Cupertino Association for Youth LGBTQ+ Advocates! Colloquially known as CAYLA, we're a diverse group of students from or related to Cupertino aiming to celebrate and empower members of the LGBTQ+ community in Cupertino.
Our goals are to:
  1. Create a more inclusive Cupertino through policies and activism.

  2. Develop a safe and local social space for the LGBTQ+ community.

  3. Celebrate the LGBTQ+ community through events and art.
